Rules

Making Predictions

For each upcoming match, you can predict the winning team and number of goals per team. You can change your prediction once you make it.

Scoring

Points are awarded for only correct predictions based on the match result and final scores.
Scenario Points Awarded
Guessing the winning team correctly 2
Guessing the winning team with the same score difference 3
Guessing the draw correctly 4
Guessing the winning team and the scores correctly 5

Leagues

Each member competes and is automatically entered in the Socceropia league. Each member can create a private league with other member on Socceropia, or by inviting non-members.
